About the band

American roots with the volume turned up.

Step back into the raw, electrifying roots of American popular music with The Digs. The band brings a lean, road-tested sound built on rock drive, blues pulse, and a little late-night swagger.

It is music made for small clubs, summer stages, and packed rooms where the chorus hits hard and the amps still feel warm.
